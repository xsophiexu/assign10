This is the UX design for Clothe, an application which helps you organize, plan, and share outfits. It lets you create a digital wardrobe with images of your clothing, and I created a prototype on Figma. There are many tags that can be automatically or manually assigned to help recommend outfits based on factors like weather and occasion.
